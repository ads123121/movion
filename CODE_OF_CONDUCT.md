# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors are expected to be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment
- insults or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing
- publishing private user material
- hostile review behavior

## Collaboration expectations

- criticize code, not people
- prefer evidence over escalation
- keep discussions focused on product quality and user impact
- treat privacy and security concerns seriously

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
